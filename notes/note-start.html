Before entering, Link read the warning at the entrance of the cave: Caution! Fire-breathing dragon ahead.

Futher and futher Link went into the cave. With each step, it grew darker. This was a very deep cave indeed.

As he traveled deeper into the cave, Link thought to himself, I hope Princess Zelda is in here.

Link finally reached Princess Zelda, but was met by a fire-breathing dragon! Luckily, Link has The Master Sword and used it to defeat the dragon and escape the cave.
